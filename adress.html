Walking

The walk up to the museum is worth the walk by itself. In the winter we recommend stingers since the roads can get quite icy.

It is possible to park your bycicle outside.

Car

Free parking outside the museum.

Public transport

When arriving with the ferry, you can take every bus at the station and it will stop just outside the museum.
